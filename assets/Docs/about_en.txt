About Dribdo

Dribdo is a social platform for sharing posts, stories, groups, channels, short videos, and direct messages.

Our mission
- Help people share useful content in a respectful, safe community.
- Give creators and communities tools to publish, discuss, and grow.

What the platform includes
- Home feed with posts, photos, and videos.
- Groups for topic communities.
- Channels for creators and organizations.
- Stories, chat, and notifications.
- Privacy and safety controls in Settings.

Safety and trust
- We invest in anti-abuse tools, reporting workflows, moderation policies, and account controls.
- We enforce rules against impersonation, harassment, and copyright abuse.

Support
- Contact support@dribdo.com for technical help, abuse reports, or account/data requests.

Thank you for being part of Dribdo.
