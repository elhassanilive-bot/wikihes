Dribdo Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dribdo collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the app and related services.

1) Information we collect
- Account data: name, username, email, phone (if provided), profile photo, bio.
- Content data: posts, stories, comments, reactions, messages, media metadata.
- Device and usage data: device type, app version, language, basic diagnostics, IP-derived region, activity logs.
- Safety data: reports, moderation decisions, blocked users, abuse signals.

2) How we use information
- Provide core features: feed, chat, groups, channels, stories, notifications.
- Improve relevance: ranking, recommendations, and anti-spam quality controls.
- Security and integrity: detect fraud, abuse, impersonation, and policy violations.
- Communication: service alerts, account notices, support responses.
- Legal compliance: enforce Terms, DMCA, and applicable laws.

3) Sharing of information
- We do not sell personal data.
- We may share data with infrastructure providers (hosting, analytics, storage) under contractual safeguards.
- We may disclose data when required by law or to protect users and platform safety.
- Public content is visible according to your privacy settings and audience choices.

4) Data retention
- We retain account and content data while your account is active.
- Some logs and safety records may be retained for a limited period for security and legal reasons.
- If you request deletion, we process it according to account deletion rules and legal obligations.

5) Your controls
- Edit profile and privacy options in Settings.
- Control who can message, tag, mention, and interact with your content.
- Manage blocked users, watch history, saved items, and notification preferences.
- Request account/data deletion from Support.

6) Security
- We apply technical and organizational safeguards to protect data.
- No system is 100% secure; users should use strong passwords and protect account credentials.

7) Children
- Dribdo is not intended for children who are below the minimum legal age in their jurisdiction.

8) International processing
- Data may be processed in different regions where our service providers operate, with safeguards where required.

9) Policy updates
- We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated in-app when appropriate.
