DMCA Policy

If you believe content on Dribdo infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA notice with:
- Your contact information.
- Identification of the copyrighted work.
- Identification of the allegedly infringing content (URL/post link).
- A statement of good-faith belief.
- A statement under penalty of perjury that the notice is accurate and you are authorized.
- Your signature (physical or electronic).

Send notices to: support@dribdo.com

Counter-notice
If your content was removed by mistake, you may submit a lawful counter-notice including:
- Your contact information.
- Identification of removed content.
- A statement under penalty of perjury of good-faith belief in error.
- Consent to jurisdiction where required by law.
- Your signature.

Repeat infringement
Dribdo may limit or terminate accounts with repeated copyright violations.
